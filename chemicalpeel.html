What is a Chemical Peel?

A chemical peel is a non-surgical cosmetic treatment that involves the application of a chemical solution to the skin. The solution works by removing the outermost layers of the skin, revealing smoother, more youthful-looking skin beneath.

Chemical peels can be used to address a range of skin concerns, including fine lines and wrinkles, uneven skin tone, sun damage, acne, and hyperpigmentation.

Chemical peels work by exfoliating the outermost layers of the skin, promoting cell turnover, and stimulating the production of collagen and elastin.
