Laser hair reduction

Laser treatments use concentrated beams of light to target specific areas of the skin or body. At Lime Aesthetics, we offer a range of laser treatments that are designed to address a variety of concerns, including hair removal, skin rejuvenation, and body contouring. Our trained and certified professionals use the latest laser technology to provide safe, effective, and non-invasive treatments that deliver long-lasting results.

What areas does laser Treatment work on?

Face: Laser treatments can be used to improve the appearance of the skin on the face, including reducing fine lines and wrinkles, minimizing pores, and improving skin texture.
Bikini area: Laser hair removal can be used to remove unwanted hair in the bikini area, leaving the skin smooth and hair-free.

Legs: Laser hair removal can be used to remove unwanted hair on the legs, providing a long-lasting and effective solution to shaving or waxing.

Underarms: Laser hair removal can be used to remove unwanted hair in the underarm area, reducing irritation and discomfort associated with shaving or waxing.

Arms: Laser treatments can be used to improve the appearance of the skin on the arms, including reducing sun damage, age spots, and minimizing the appearance of veins.

Chest and neck: Laser treatments can be used to improve the appearance of the skin on the chest and neck, reducing fine lines and wrinkles and improving skin texture.

Back: Laser hair removal can be used to remove unwanted hair on the back, providing a long-lasting and effective solution to shaving or waxing.

Hands: Laser treatments can be used to improve the appearance of the skin on the hands, reducing age spots, minimizing the appearance of veins, and improving skin texture.
