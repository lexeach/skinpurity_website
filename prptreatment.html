What is Platelet Therapy (PRP)?

Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy is a medical treatment that utilizes a small sample of your own blood to create a concentrated solution of platelets, growth factors, and other healing components. This solution is then injected into the targeted area to stimulate natural healing and regeneration.

PRP therapy is a safe and effective treatment option for a range of cosmetic concerns, including wrinkles, fine lines, acne scars, and hair loss.
