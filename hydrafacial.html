Hydra Facial

Hydrafacials are a popular non-invasive skincare treatment that can help improve the overall appearance and health of the skin. It uses a unique patented technology to cleanse, exfoliate, extract impurities, and hydrate the skin all at once. One of the popular add-on features that Lime Aesthetics offers is LED Therapy, which involves using specific wavelengths of light to penetrate the skin at various depths to promote healing, reduce inflammation and stimulate collagen production.

What are the Benefits of hydrafacials?

Cleanses and exfoliates: It use a patented vortex-fusion technology to cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate the skin. This gentle and non-invasive process removes dead skin cells, unclogs pores, and revitalizes the skin for a more youthful and radiant appearance.

Hydrates the skin: One of the unique features of HydraFacials is that they infuse the skin with a serum containing antioxidants, peptides, and hyaluronic acid. This helps to hydrate the skin and improve its overall health, leaving it soft and smooth to the touch.

Reduces fine lines and wrinkles: The exfoliation and hydration provided by HydraFacials can help to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. This can help to give the skin a more youthful and refreshed appearance.
Reduces hyperpigmentation: It can also help to reduce hyperpigmentation caused by sun damage, aging, and other factors. By removing dead skin cells and infusing the skin with antioxidants, HydraFacials can help to even out skin tone and reduce the appearance of dark spots.

Helps to control acne: It can help to control acne and prevent future breakouts. By removing excess oil and unclogging pores, HydraFacials can help to reduce inflammation and promote healthy skin. 

Who Should Not Get a Hydrafacial and Why?
